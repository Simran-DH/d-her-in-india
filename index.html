What's it like being a D(Her) in India ?!

Definitions of resilience, in the words of women practising Digital Humanities in India.

Add your definition

About. This archive gathers reflections shared by women Digital Humanities scholars and practitioners in India, in response to the question: what does resilience mean to you in your DH practice? Each voice appears here with the attribution its contributor chose, including the choice to remain anonymous.

Add your voice. If you are a woman working in or alongside DH in India, we would love to include your definition. Use the button above to contribute; responses are reviewed before publication.

Data. The definitions displayed here are openly available as a CSV dataset, shared with each contributor's consent.
