What's it like being a D(Her) in India ?!

Resilience, in the words of women practising Digital Humanities in India.

Add your voice

About. This archive gathers reflections shared by women Digital Humanities scholars and practitioners in India, in answer to questions about what resilience means in their DH practice. Each voice appears here with the attribution its contributor chose, including the choice to remain anonymous.

Add your voice. If you are a woman working in or alongside DH in India, we would love to include your reflections. Use the button above to contribute; responses are reviewed before publication.

Credits. The form of this site pays homage to Jason Heppler's What Is Digital Humanities?
